Keep this tab open and the screen unlocked (dimmed screen is fine with "Keep screen awake" on) — iOS suspends audio timers once the screen actually locks. Turning the mute switch off on the side of the phone will still let chimes sound through. Chimes fire once per hour, on the hour, and are skipped entirely during your Silent Hours window.
